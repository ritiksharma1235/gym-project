Yoga

Yoga is a mind-body practice combining physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation to promote flexibility, strength, and mental well-being. Originating in ancient India, yoga aims to balance body, mind, and spirit. It includes various styles like Hatha, Vinyasa, and Ashtanga, each with different focuses, from relaxation to intense physical challenges.

Power Lifting

Powerlifting is a strength sport focused on three main lifts: the squat, bench press, and deadlift. Athletes aim to lift the maximum possible weight in each of these lifts, with three attempts per lift in competition. Powerlifting emphasizes raw strength and is categorized by weight classes, allowing individuals of various body sizes to compete.

Cardio

Cardio, short for cardiovascular exercise, involves activities that raise the heart rate and improve the efficiency of the heart, lungs, and circulatory system. Common forms of cardio include running, cycling, swimming, and dancing. It helps burn calories, improves endurance, and enhances overall cardiovascular health.

Personal Trainer

A gym personal trainer is a certified fitness professional who helps individuals achieve their health and fitness goals through personalized exercise programs, guidance, and motivation. They assess a client's fitness level, create tailored workout plans, provide instruction on proper exercise techniques, and offer nutritional advice.
